Featured Mods

Curated list of community-made mods.

The following is a list of the best that the modding community has to offer, curated by Fuji maintainers:

Maps

The Bee-Sides by wiedo

A wonderful map pack by wiedo and friends, featuring 14 strawberries and 8 cassettes for a total of 22 berries!

Gameplay

Super Mario 64 Mechanics by psyGamer21

Replaces the game's default movement mechanics with that of Mario 64.

Features

Discord Rich Presence by aliteralpotato

Adds Discord rich presence support to Celeste 64. Show your friends what you're playing!

deltarune explosion by aliteralpotato

Replaces certain animations with the explosion from Deltarune.
